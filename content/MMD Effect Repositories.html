MMD Visual Effects Introduction

TMMD special effects can help users enhance the quality of the picture or more unique picture style.

渲染器类

Renderer Class


像素风格特效器

PixelStyle Effects

这个渲染器使用默认的粗边无阴影的模型材质进行渲染，使用者可以通过控制器来开启阴影渲染或是屏幕空间采样。

This renderer uses the default thick edge shadowless model material for rendering. Users can turn on shadow rendering or screen space sampling through the controller.

Get Effect



Karl 2 PBR渲染器

PixelStyle Effects

这个渲染器采用基于物理的渲染方式，支持用户使用法线贴图，环境闭塞贴图，自发光贴图，次表面贴图，金属性贴图，漫反射贴图，细节贴图，粗糙度贴图。并且内置了屏幕空间反射，超采样反锯齿等效果器。

This renderer adopts physical based rendering mode, and supports users to use Normal map, AO map, Emissive map, Subsurface map, Metalness map, Albedo map and Roughness map. And built-in screen space reflection, super sampling anti aliasing and other effects.
